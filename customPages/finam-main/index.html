My Role

Finam is a top-3 brokerage firm in Russia and ranks second among providers of individual investment accounts in the country. Finam provides a wide range of financial services to its customers from banking products to various investment opportunities.

I worked on the company’s main digital service. I collaborated with a product owner and four development teams and communicated with the company board stakeholders. There wasn’t a centralized UX team in the company, so I directly communicated with customers, users, and other stakeholders.


Previous  design

Finam has a pool of customers who use different offerings such as banking products, brokerage services, or investment packages. Historically, the UI of every new feature was thrown into in one web portal without thinking about the overall information architecture so the navigation was quite complicated. 

When I got the task to redesign this, there wasn’t a clear understanding in the company of what exactly should be changed. And I started by defining the main UX flaws of the web service.

Design Process in an Enterprise Company

Working on the main customer application in an enterprise company has many challenges. Many different opinions, expectations, and the complicated company structure — that’s just a few examples. I started the Discovery by having a couple of workshops and many personal meetings with top management stakeholders.

Observing Customer Experience

Finam’s HQ had the customer office and education center in the same building. It gave me all-day access to the company’s customers.

How To Be Less Biased?

The stakeholders often provided direct recommendations and solutions. In order not to be biased and back up my solutions, I checked  hypotheses and collected data about our customers.

Main Findings

The Navigation and Shorcuts were the most confusing things and
they needed to be redesigned first.

10+ User Types With Different Goals

There are 4 user groups and each group has up to 5 user types.

CJM: What Our Users Actually Do

For the most important user types, I built user personas and CJMs. It helped to talk with the business stakeholders about users and their needs (and not just about UI).

Competitor Analysis

Checking our main competitor solutions gave the following:
— see how competitors dealt with the same problems
— see the big picture of the market
— find features and solutions that we didn’t even think

Why Makes Us Different

Another very valuable document is the User Experience Values. It helped to maintain the same language during conversations with the stakeholders and developers.

Solution: New Navigation and Customized UX 

Eash user type is going to have a unique set of widgets that is relevant only to them.

Prototype Testing

I conducted 3 iterations of prototype testing with the main user types including both new and experienced users. 

Final Design

The final designs had the updated styles, UI kit, and all needed specifications.
